## 9. Market Making Infrastructure

### Professional Market Making Guide

**Overview**: Market making on prediction markets has become highly competitive in 2026, with professional capital compressing spreads from 3-5% (2024) to 1-2% (2026).

#### Infrastructure Requirements

**Minimum Specifications**:
- **Server**: Trading VPS in Amsterdam (Polymarket) or NYC (Kalshi)
- **Latency**: <10ms to exchange (<1ms ideal)
- **CPU**: 4+ cores (8+ for HFT)
- **RAM**: 8GB+ (16GB+ recommended)
- **Network**: 1 Gbps+ (10 Gbps for HFT)
- **Uptime**: 99.9%+ SLA

**Software Stack**:
- Language: Python (predmarket library) or Rust (performance)
- Database: PostgreSQL or ClickHouse for analytics
- Monitoring: Custom dashboards, alert systems
- Risk Management: Real-time position tracking

#### Speed & Latency Considerations

**Why Speed Matters**:
- News breaks → prices must adjust instantly
- Fastest cancellation of stale orders = fewer adverse fills
- Arbitrage opportunities last milliseconds-seconds
- Order priority in CLOB (Central Limit Order Book)

**Latency Hierarchy**:
| Setup | Latency | Competitive? |
|-------|---------|--------------|
| Home internet | 50-200ms | ❌ No |
| Generic VPS | 10-50ms | ⚠️ Limited |
| Trading VPS (local) | 5-10ms | ✅ Yes |
| Ultra-low latency VPS | <1ms | ✅✅ Ideal |
| Colocated server | <0.5ms | ✅✅✅ HFT only |

**Performance Benchmark** (QuantVPS Amsterdam):
- Polymarket execution: 0.52ms average
- Order cancellation: <1ms
- Market data receipt: Real-time WebSocket (<5ms)

#### Market Making Strategies

**Basic Spread Capture**:
- Post limit orders on both sides
- Earn spread when both fill
- Current spreads: 1-2% (compressed)

**Dynamic Spread Adjustment**:
- Widen spreads during volatility
- Tighten during calm periods
- Monitor inventory risk

**Inventory Management**:
- Set maximum position limits
- Hedge with correlated markets
- Cross-platform hedging (Polymarket ↔ Kalshi)

**News-Driven Adjustment**:
- Real-time news integration (Adjacent News API)
- Cancel stale quotes instantly on catalysts
- Re-price based on new information

#### Risk Management

**Position Limits**:
- Maximum exposure per market: 5-10% of capital
- Maximum total exposure: 50-70% of capital
- Reserve 30-50% for hedging/opportunities

**Stop-Loss Protocols**:
- Automated position reduction at thresholds
- Market correlation monitoring
- Black swan event detection

**Regulatory Considerations**:
- Kalshi: CFTC-regulated (US legal)
- Polymarket: Crypto-native (verify local laws)
- Tax implications: Consult professional
